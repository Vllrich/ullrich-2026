# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Technical founders, CTOs, and engineering managers (25-50, DACH/EU + international) evaluating senior technical leadership for hire or advisory. They arrive skeptical - they have seen dozens of generic consulting sites and know the tells. They spend 90 seconds deciding whether this is worth a conversation.

## Product Purpose

Personal brand site for Lars Ullrich: generate qualified leads for fractional CTO/CAIO engagements, AI production builds, and strategic advisory. Success means a visitor who did not know Lars arrives at the contact form convinced he understands their specific problem - because the site demonstrated it rather than claimed it.

## Brand Personality

Precise. Practiced. Direct. Voice of earned confidence - never performance. The kind of person who understates because the work speaks. References a creative past without romanticizing it. The unusual technical biography (stage to GPU to AI) is a differentiator, not a distraction.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages: gradient heroes, icon+card grids, floating UI mockups
- Consulting-brochure aesthetics: heavy serifs, navy/gold palette, handshake energy
- Agency-showcase sites: too much animation, dense layout, self-congratulatory copy
- AI-hype templates: purple/teal gradients, buzzword stacking, capability theater
- The 2026 warm-cream editorial default: already saturated, reads as AI-made

## Design Principles

1. Practice what you preach. A site about production AI should feel engineered, not decorated.
2. Evidence, not claims. Every statement earns a proof point: a project, a company, a constraint.
3. Economy of means. Typography does the work. Nothing decorative that does not carry information.
4. The creative past is a differentiator, not a portfolio. Show it, then get out of the way.
5. Confident asymmetry. A site assembled from a template signals the same about the person.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Body text at 4.5:1 contrast minimum. Reduced-motion alternative for all animations. Keyboard-navigable forms with visible focus states. Semantic HTML throughout.
